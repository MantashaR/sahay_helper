Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) — Operational Guidelines
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India

1. INTRODUCTION
PM-KISAN is a Central Sector Scheme launched on 1st February 2019 to provide income support to all landholding farmer families in the country. Under the scheme, financial benefit of Rs. 6,000 per year is transferred directly in three equal instalments of Rs. 2,000 each, every four months, into the bank accounts of beneficiary farmer families through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode.

2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
2.1 The scheme is open to all landholding farmer families, defined as a family comprising husband, wife and minor children, who collectively own cultivable land as per land records of the concerned State/Union Territory.
2.2 The following categories of beneficiaries of higher economic status shall NOT be eligible:
  (a) All Institutional Land holders.
  (b) Farmer families holding constitutional posts.
  (c) Former and present Ministers/State Ministers and former/present Members of LokSabha/RajyaSabha/State Legislative Assemblies/State Legislative Councils, former and present Mayors of Municipal Corporations, former and present Chairpersons of District Panchayats.
  (d) All serving or retired officers and employees of Central/State Government Ministries/Offices/Departments and its field units, Central or State PSEs and Attached offices/Autonomous Institutions under Government, as well as regular employees of the Local Bodies (Excluding Multi Tasking Staff/Class IV/Group D employees).
  (e) All superannuated/retired pensioners whose monthly pension is Rs. 10,000 or more (Excluding Multi Tasking Staff/Class IV/Group D employees).
  (f) All Persons who paid Income Tax in the last assessment year.
  (g) Professionals like Doctors, Engineers, Lawyers, Chartered Accountants, and Architects registered with Professional bodies and carrying out profession by undertaking practices.

3. APPLICATION PROCESS
3.1 Self-registration is available at pmkisan.gov.in. The farmer needs to provide Aadhaar number, bank account details, and land record information.
3.2 Registration can also be done through Common Service Centres (CSC) or the local revenue/agriculture officer at the village level.
3.3 NO FEES are payable to any agent. Applications via official channels are completely free.

4. PAYMENT OF BENEFIT
4.1 The benefit will be transferred directly to the bank account of the beneficiary.
4.2 Aadhaar seeding of bank account is mandatory.
4.3 Three instalments of Rs. 2,000 are released in April-July, August-November, and December-March.
