Ayushman Bharat — Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY)
National Health Authority, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

1. SCHEME OVERVIEW
Ayushman Bharat PMJAY is the largest health assurance scheme in the world, aiming to provide a health cover of Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization to over 12 crore poor and vulnerable families (approximately 55 crore beneficiaries).

2. ELIGIBILITY
2.1 Beneficiaries are identified based on deprivation and occupational criteria from the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011. The scheme covers families that meet at least one of the following criteria for rural areas:
  - Households with only one room with kuchha walls and kuchha roof
  - Households with no adult member in the age group of 16-59
  - Female-headed households with no adult male member in the age group of 16-59
  - Disabled member and no able-bodied adult member in the family
  - SC/ST households
  - Landless households deriving major part of their income from manual casual labour
2.2 For urban areas, occupational categories such as ragpickers, beggars, domestic workers, street vendors, construction workers, sanitation workers, sweepers, etc. are eligible.
2.3 EXPANSION 2024: All senior citizens aged 70 years and above are now eligible regardless of income, under the Ayushman Vaya Vandana initiative.

3. BENEFITS
3.1 Cashless and paperless access to services at empanelled hospitals, both public and private.
3.2 Coverage of pre-hospitalization (up to 3 days) and post-hospitalization (up to 15 days) expenses including diagnostics and medicines.
3.3 No restriction on family size, age, or gender.
3.4 All pre-existing conditions are covered from day one.
3.5 Covers 1,949 medical procedures including treatment of cancer, cardiology, neurosurgery, orthopaedics, etc.

4. HOW TO APPLY / GET YOUR AYUSHMAN CARD
4.1 Verify eligibility at pmjay.gov.in or by calling the helpline 14555.
4.2 Visit any empanelled hospital or Common Service Centre with Aadhaar to get your Ayushman card.
4.3 No fees are charged for issuing the Ayushman card. Beware of fake "Ayushman Mitras" charging money outside hospitals — report them to 14555.

5. WHO IS NOT ELIGIBLE
Households with motorised two/three/four wheelers, mechanised farming equipment, Kisan Credit Card with credit limit above Rs. 50,000, government employees, those with monthly income above Rs. 10,000, those with refrigerators/landlines, and those owning more than 2.5 acres of irrigated land.
